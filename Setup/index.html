    Initial Setup

    The Paypal-Merchant client id, helps you to enable this experience on your app and collect consent from Users that they would like to opt in and use this feature in your app when they pay with paypal.

    How to create a merchant app on our developer portal

    1. Go to https://developer.paypal.com/developer/applications/

    2. Add a name and click on create app

    3. You will see the Merchant client_id generated in the next page.

    4. In App settings section, select In-app Checkout

    5. Add the universal link you created on your domain in the return url section.

    6. Finally mail our team for adding a special scope on your client ID to get the Native Checkout experience. You can mail us here
